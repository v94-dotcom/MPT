MPT reminds you every few days to type your master password and email from memory, verifying correctness against a securely stored hash.

Your password is hashed with Argon2id and can never be recovered — not even by this app. Emails are encrypted with AES-256-GCM via Android Keystore. The app has zero internet permissions and never connects to any network.

Features:
- Periodic password recall challenges
- Streak tracking and verification history
- Adaptive difficulty scaling
- Home screen widgets
- Encrypted backup and restore
- Biometric app lock
- Material You dynamic theming
